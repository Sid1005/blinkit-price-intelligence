# Returns & Refund Policy (Demo Marketplace)

This is a demonstration policy used to ground complaint triage. It mirrors common
Indian marketplace policies but is not a real, binding policy. Complaint triage cites
this document and never issues binding decisions; uncertain cases require human
confirmation.

## Return windows

- **Electronics (phones, laptops, audio):** 7-day replacement-only window for defects,
  damage on delivery, or wrong item. No refund-only for opened electronics unless dead
  on arrival (DOA).
- **Groceries & perishables:** Returns accepted only for **expired**, **near-expiry**
  (less than 30% shelf life left), **damaged**, or **wrong** items. Report within 24
  hours of delivery with a photo.
- **Personal care & FMCG (sealed):** 7-day return if unopened and seal intact.
- **Fashion:** 10-day return for size/quality issues.

## Refund timelines

- Prepaid orders: refund to source in **5–7 business days** after pickup/QC.
- UPI refunds: typically **3–5 business days**.
- COD orders: refund to bank account / wallet in **7–10 business days** after the
  customer shares verified bank details.
- A refund flagged as "delayed" beyond these windows should be **escalated**.

## Damaged / wrong / expired items

- Eligible for **free pickup** and **full refund or replacement**.
- Require a photo of the item and the invoice/packaging.
- Expired perishables are always eligible for a full refund.

## Fake / counterfeit product claims

- Treat as **high severity**. Do not confirm or deny authenticity automatically.
- Collect the order ID, photos of the product, batch/serial number, and packaging.
- **Escalate** to the brand-authorised verification team. Mark the resolution as
  requiring confirmation; never auto-issue a refund for counterfeit claims.

## COD amount disputes

- If the customer was charged more than the app-displayed price, request the invoice
  and the amount paid. If the overcharge is confirmed, the **difference is refundable**.
- Underpayment or partial COD collection is escalated to the logistics partner.

## Non-returnable

- Opened consumables (food once opened), intimate/personal hygiene items, and
  customised products are non-returnable unless defective.
