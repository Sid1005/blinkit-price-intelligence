# Indian E-commerce Festival & Sale Calendar (Demo)

This calendar drives festival-aware deal conditioning. Dates and discount bands are
curated demo values, not live promotions.

## Major sale events

- **Republic Day Sale (January):** Electronics and large appliances see 15–22% off.
  Amazon Great Republic Day Sale and Flipkart Republic Day Sale dominate.
- **Holi (March):** Modest 8–12% off on groceries, colours, and sweets.
- **Summer Sale (May):** Cooling appliances (ACs, coolers, fridges) discounted 10–18%.
- **Independence Day Sale (August):** Freedom Sale / Big Saving Days; 18–25% on phones
  and electronics.
- **Onam (September):** Strong in Kerala; appliances and gold see 12–16% off.
- **Big Billion Days (October, Flipkart):** Flagship sale; 25–35% on electronics, the
  best phone prices of the year typically land here.
- **Great Indian Festival (October, Amazon.in):** Amazon's flagship; matches Big
  Billion Days, 25–35% on phones, TVs, laptops.
- **Dhanteras (October/November):** Gold, silver, utensils, and appliances; 18–25% off.
- **Diwali (November):** Peak gifting; 22–30% off across categories, bank card offers
  stack on top.
- **Black Friday (late November):** Growing in India; 20–28% on electronics and fashion.
- **Year End Sale (December):** Clearance of old inventory; 12–18% off.

## Buy / wait / avoid heuristics

- If the current or next month contains **Big Billion Days, Great Indian Festival, or
  Diwali**, advise **WAIT** for non-urgent electronics — discounts of 25%+ are likely.
- If the observed price is already **below the festival-adjusted floor** (more than the
  expected festival discount off MRP), advise **BUY NOW**.
- If a price **rose** versus the trailing months and stock is low, that is a
  **demand spike** — advise **AVOID** paying the premium unless urgent.
- Groceries and staples have thin festival discounts (under 12%); timing matters less,
  so judge mainly on unit price versus MRP.

## Bank and platform offers

- Festival prices often stack a 10% instant bank discount (HDFC/ICICI/SBI/Axis).
- No-cost EMI is common on phones and laptops above ₹15,000 during festivals.
- COD is widely available but some festival deals are prepaid-only.
